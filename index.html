Quiz!

Quizzes are like a game in which the specific knowledge of the players is tested by a series of questions. Each quiz consists of a list of multiple choice questions. There are a number of possible answers to each question, and it's up to you to decide which one is correct.
Just like a game, you receive a score for the number of questions you answered correctly. Try again and again to try to beat your previous high score, or the scores of others.
